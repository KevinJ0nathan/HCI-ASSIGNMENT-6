Frequently Asked Questions

What Is Onigiri?

Onigiri is an online learning web portal affiliated with the Kouyou Language Institute, offering a diverse range of recorded courses and interactive quizzes. As part of the Kouyou Language Institute group, Onigiri aims to provide an engaging and comprehensive online learning experience for its users.

When Was Onigiri Found?

Onigiri, the online learning web portal, was founded on September 12, 2023, by three individuals: Adrian, Jonathan, and Kevin Jonathan. This relatively new platform is driven by a commitment to delivering quality content, and its founders emphasize the importance of learning from experience. As part of their approach, Onigiri, affiliated with the Kouyou Language Institute group, aims to provide an enriching online learning experience for users, drawing on the collective expertise of its founders.

Why Use Onigiri?

Onigiri offers a distinct Japanese learning experience, blending curated courses, practical learning, and a supportive community. Its founders' expertise drives an inclusive platform with adaptable schedules and quality content. It stands as an interactive, innovative, and user-focused space for authentic language mastery.
